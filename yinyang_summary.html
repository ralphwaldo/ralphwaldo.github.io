Yin-Yang Rules

Colour all the cells one of two colours (or, shade some cells but not others), such that:

  • all cells of one colour are orthogonally connected
  • all cells of the other colour are orthogonally connected
  • no 2x2 block consists entirely of cells of one colour
First secret

No 2x2 block of cells can show a checkerboard pattern

Second secret

The border cells cannot be split into four separate blocks of colours

Third secret (odd grids)

The number of edge cells of each colour is odd (if the border cells are not all the same colour)

Third secret (even grids)

The number of edge cells of each colour is even (and the border must be split into two colour blocks)

Additionally the blocks of edge cells of one colour adjacent to corner cells are also even in length
